RESEARCH AREA

The ambit of Clinical Research at CareVista Superspecialty Hospital will cover Phase II / III/IV studies with different indication like Metastatic Breast Cancer, Non Small Cell Lung Cancer, Ovarian Cancer, COVID and POST COVID indication, Nephrological studies. CareVista is well equipped with the highest standards for Clinical research purposes with high quality local laboratories facilities.

Chronic Kidney Disease

Chronic Kidney disease means gradual loss of kidney function. Nowadays this is a common disorder to Indian Population. Here we are conducting Phase III studies with this indication.

Non Small cell Lung Cancer

Lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer related mortality world-wide and amongst males in India. About 85% of lung cancers are non-small cell lung cancers (NSCLC).

Interventional Radiology

Interventional Radiology is a sub-specialty of Radiology that utilizes various imaging techniques to guide percutaneous minimally invasive procedures for both diagnosis and therapy.

COVID

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. During this pandemic situation the dedicated research team of CareVista provides their effort for clinical trials also. Here Phases II, III, IV studies are going on.
